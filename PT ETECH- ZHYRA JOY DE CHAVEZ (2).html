About Me

I am a Grade 11 student from Fidelis Senior High School who enjoys expressing my creativity through dance and visual art. These activities allow me to share my emotions, ideas, and personality in a unique and meaningful way. As the eldest child in my family, I have learned to be responsible, independent, and dependable at a young age. Although I am naturally introverted, I find confidence and self-expression through dancing and creating art. These passions not only help me relax and enjoy my time but also allow me to grow as a person by improving my creativity, patience, and dedication. Through my experiences, I continue to discover more about myself and strive to develop my skills while balancing my studies and personal interests.
